# Planning Seed — AI Biosecurity Pillar (deferred)

**Status:** Planning seed. Run `/brainstorm` next session to develop into full spec.
**Driver:** External strategy doc bucket F (AI-biosecurity, asymmetric sleeper).

## Why

The strategy argues that AI accelerates biology, biology is a clear existential-risk domain, and governments will fund countermeasures. That maps to a distinct ticker pillar with its own signal sources — different from the AI infra/robotics/cyber pillars we already cover.

## Scope sketch (to brainstorm)

**Candidate tickers (8-10):**
- ILMN (Illumina) — sequencing platforms
- ONT.L (Oxford Nanopore) — long-read sequencing
- TWST (Twist Bioscience) — DNA synthesis
- DNA (Ginkgo Bioworks) — synthetic biology platform
- ABSI (Absci) — generative protein design
- RXRX (Recursion Pharmaceuticals) — AI drug discovery
- SDGR (Schrödinger) — physics-based drug discovery
- MRNA (Moderna) — mRNA platform (rapid vaccine response capability)
- BNTX (BioNTech) — same thesis as MRNA

**Pillar structure decision (open):**
- Single layer `biosecurity` (8-10 tickers) — simple
- OR split into `biosecurity_platforms` (ILMN, ONT, TWST, DNA) + `biosecurity_ai_drug` (ABSI, RXRX, SDGR) + `biosecurity_pandemic` (MRNA, BNTX) — more nuanced

**New signal sources (to design):**
- FDA approval announcements (RSS feed)
- Sequencing cost curve (Illumina earnings + NIH cost-per-genome time series)
- DNA synthesis screening mandates (regulatory)
- Wastewater surveillance contracts
- Government biodefense funding (BARDA budget lines via SAM.gov subset)
- AI-bio model capability evals (METR/Apollo Research releases)

**Risk assessment to make explicit in spec:**
- Biotech cycles are brutal — RXRX, SDGR, ABSI all down 70-90% over 24 months
- Public biosecurity market is small
- Many companies are science projects, not businesses
- Recommend small allocation cap (e.g., max 5% of layer-weighted positions)

**Scope boundary:**
- Out: large pharma (PFE, MRK, JNJ) — too diversified, weak AI-bio signal
- Out: Benchling, Asimov, SecureBio (private — add to PENDING_IPO_WATCHLIST instead)
